# Cross Family Grouping Boundary

## Summary

Some services span multiple research families or sit near more than one platform domain. Cross-family grouping should preserve all plausible labels and the evidence for each label instead of forcing a single family.

## Context

- Related controller note: `knowledge/platform/controller-family-backlog.md`
- Related topology note: `knowledge/platform/topology-vocabulary.md`
- Related candidate note: `knowledge/platform/candidate-joins.md`
- Queue cycle: `planning/PLATFORM_SESSION_QUEUE.md` cycle 242

## Guidance

- Store grouping reason beside the group label.
- Allow multiple labels when evidence spans buses, virtual providers, or user-space adjacency.
- Keep unknown groups explicit instead of inventing family names.
