# Attachment Glossary

## Summary

Attachment is a provider/client relationship in the registry lifecycle. It is stronger than a candidate join, but it is still not the same as successful startup or useful driver behavior.

## Context

- Related provider note: `knowledge/platform/provider-discovery-vs-startup.md`
- Related matching note: `knowledge/platform/matching-path.md`
- Related service note: `knowledge/iokit/IOService.md`
- Queue cycle: `planning/PLATFORM_SESSION_QUEUE.md` cycle 261

## Definition

Attachment: the relationship where a client service is entered into a registry plane as attached to a provider service.

## Boundary

- Candidate join: possible relationship.
- Attachment: observed registry relationship.
- Startup: selected service begins runtime role.
